Adventure tour

As a kind of tourism in India, adventure tourism has recently grown in India. This involves exploration of remote areas and exotic locales and engaging in various activities. For adventure tourism in India, tourists prefer to go for trekking to places like Ladakh, Sikkim, and Himalaya.

Wildlife tour

​​India has a rich forest cover which has some beautiful and exotic species of
wildlife – some of which that are even endangered and very rare. This has
boosted wildlife tourism in India. The places where a foreign tourist can go
for wildlife tourism in India are the Sariska Wildlife Sanctuary, Keoladeo
Ghana National Park (Rajasthan), and Corbett National Park (Uttarkhand).
